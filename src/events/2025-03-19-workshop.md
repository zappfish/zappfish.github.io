**Workshop goals and content:**

The goals of this workshop was to introduce the concept of a "data model" and gather all the information required to represent zebrafish toxicology phenotype data.

By using real data generated by participants and through hands-on exercises, we identified all information required to report, understand, and reuse/reproduce toxicological assays. This information focused on   
  *    the toxicological exposure or treatment
  *    phenotype observed after exposure or treatment

We also explored the ways community members would submit data, in order to plan how to create the data submission platform.
