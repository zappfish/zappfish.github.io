**Workshop goals and content:**
* introduced the concept and importance of a data model 

* tested the draft data model for zebrafish toxicology phenotype data, which includes
  *    information about the treated fish 
  *    toxicological exposure or treatment
  *    phenotype observed after treatment

* reviewed the data model to confirm it captures all required information for reproducibility

* explored how community members would submit data

* presented multiple options for the official project name and asked participants to vote
