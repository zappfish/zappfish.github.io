**Workshop goals and content:**
* We conducted exercises to identify the pieces of information needed for reporting phenotypes associated with toxicology or environmental exposure.

* We presented and tested the first version of the toxicology-phenotype data model.

* We reviewed the Zebrafish Phenotype Ontology and presented options for using it as a standard for phenotype reporting.

* We presented several options for the official project name and asked participants to vote.
